Hello👋

I am Farhana Rupom, a professional speaker, entrepreneur and influencer from Bangladesh. With more than 15 years of experience in business and public speaking, I have developed a reputation for my ability to inspire and motivate audiences globally. I am sharing my encouraging stories to the world to enthuse people. I am “The Futurist Ambassador”.

As a member of Bangladesh Professional Speakers, I am passionate about helping individuals and businesses to unlock their full potential. I am specialised in leadership, distribution and retail sales, communication, and personal development and also have helped numerous organisations and individuals to achieve their goals by using my skills and experiences..

Experience

I have fifteen years of experience in sales and distribution in the top telecommunication company "Telenor" in Bangladesh. Throughout my career, I worked in different corners of the country, primarily rural areas, where I have experienced diversified social contexts and got the opportunity to work closely in transforming rural life to be connected and grow financially. I lead a sales team of over 2000 people, 5000 km run by a month. I am the Women's Chapter President of the Bangladesh Professional Speakers Association. I am a Sales Trainer, Leadership Coach, Professional Speaker and Inspiring Storyteller who loves encouraging women to thrive from struggles and inspiring them to speak up. I guide young women to see the future beyond their expectations. I am known as the Futurist Ambassador.

According to my clients, as a leadership coach, I have always successfully provided the proper guideline to boost the business growth and I am happy to see the implementation of my ideas in retail markets. In addition to my work as a speaker, I am an accomplished entrepreneur. I have founded several successful businesses in a variety of industries, including building development sectors. I have a project “Six Stars”. Around the year I donated 6 individuals for the betterment of their living and changing their Lives. Through my entrepreneurial ventures, I have gained a deep understanding of the challenges and opportunities faced by businesses in Bangladesh and beyond.
